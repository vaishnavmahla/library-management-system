About Us

Welcome to Athaneum, a modern library experience designed to save your time and enhance your reading journey. We understand that finding books in a library can be time-consuming, especially for students who often don’t know where to look.

That's why we've reimagined the process: browse and select your desired books right from our website. Once you've made your selection, our dedicated staff will locate the books for you, making them ready for pickup.

All you need to do is visit our library in Bandra, Mumbai to collect your books. It's simple, smart, and efficient. While returns still require a visit, the time you save during book issuance makes a big difference.

Athaneum is here to make your library visits shorter and more purposeful, giving you more time to focus on what matters—reading and learning.
